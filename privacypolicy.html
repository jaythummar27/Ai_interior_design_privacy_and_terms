Introduction

Welcome to StageCraft — AI Interior Design (the "App"), package com.jaystudio.ai.home.interior.design, developed and operated by LogicGridApps ("we," "us," "our"), located in Gujarat, India. The App uses artificial intelligence to generate interior design visualizations from your room photos.

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. Please read it carefully before using the App.

Information We Collect

Section 02

We collect information in three ways: data you provide manually, data generated automatically from your device, and data collected by third-party SDKs we integrate (detailed in Section 8).

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Room photos — images you upload or capture to generate AI interior designs
  • Design preferences — room type, style, color palette, design mode, and optional custom text prompts

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

The following are collected automatically when you use certain features:

  • Device identifier — persistent ID used to manage AI credits in Firebase Firestore; no account registration required
  • App settings — theme, font scale, and font family stored locally
  • Design history — metadata and image references stored locally (Room database)
  • Purchase information — processed by Google Play and RevenueCat (no full card details stored by us)
  • Advertising identifiers — collected by Google AdMob when ads are shown — see Section 5
  • App usage events — collected by Firebase Analytics — see Section 6
  • Device information — model, OS version, language — collected by Firebase and AdMob
  • Remote configuration — Firebase Remote Config feature flags (no personal content in requests)

2.3 Data NOT Collected by Us

LogicGridApps does not require your name, email, phone number, or Google account sign-in for core App use. We do not sell your personal information.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Section 05
AdMob Disclosure: This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data from your device to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This section explains how advertising works in the App.

Ad Formats Used

⚡ Interstitial Ads Full-screen rewarded interstitial ads when you close the paywall without purchasing (when enabled). Frequency is throttled via Firebase Remote Config to limit disruption.
🎬 Rewarded Video Ads Other ad formats may be disabled via Remote Config; premium or configured builds may skip paywall-dismiss ads. Watching is never mandatory.

Data Collected by AdMob

Google AdMob independently collects the following data for ad serving and measurement purposes:

  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size, language)
  • IP address (used to infer approximate geographic location)
  • App interaction signals (ad impressions, clicks)
  • Coarse location (if device grants location permission to the OS)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. LogicGridApps does not have access to or control over AdMob's data collection.

Ad Personalization

By default, AdMob may serve personalized ads based on your interests and prior app activity. You can opt out of personalized ads at any time:

  • On your Android device: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Or reset your Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID
  • For EU/EEA users: The App implements Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) for consent management where required by law

Ad Throttling & User Experience

We use Firebase Remote Config to control ad frequency (minimum interval between ads, maximum per session, honeymoon period for new users). Ads are never shown during sensitive actions such as generating a design.

For Google's data practices related to advertising, see: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Privacy FAQ.

Analytics & Remote Configuration

Section 06

Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This helps us improve features and fix issues. Firebase Analytics automatically collects:

  • Screen views and navigation events
  • App open/close and session duration
  • Device type, OS version, app version, language
  • Crash and error reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

No personally identifiable financial data (room photos or design prompts) is ever sent to Firebase Analytics.

Firebase Remote Config

We use Firebase Remote Config to dynamically control app behavior without requiring an update — including ad frequency limits, feature flags (enabling/disabling create flow, edit design, paywall, style preload, etc.), and AI credit economy settings. No personal data is transmitted to Remote Config.
